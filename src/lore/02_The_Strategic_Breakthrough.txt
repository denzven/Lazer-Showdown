# The Strategic Breakthrough

For months, Command viewed "Lazer Showdown" as a mere pastime for the alien race. They were wrong.

## The Intelligence
A young cryptographer noticed a terrifying correlation: the placement of the "shiny shells" on the alien game boards matched the exact coordinates of their heavy cruiser ship placements in orbit. The game wasn't a distraction; it was a **real-time tactical simulation**.

## The Currency of Knowledge
By recreating the game with mirrors and rudimentary lasers, human soldiers began to "play along" with the enemy. By decoding the game's logic, they predicted the alien flanking maneuvers three days before they occurred. Knowledge became the ultimate currency, and for the first time, humanity wasn't fighting blind.

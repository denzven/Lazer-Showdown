# Contender: Zlorooklp

**Rank:** Veteran Scout (Retired)
**Difficulty:** Easy

## Profile
Zlorooklp was one of the first aliens to play a friendly match with a human soldier after the ceasefire. He views Lazer Showdown as a casual hobby rather than a strict military exercise.

## Playstyle
He is forgiving and often experiments with sub-optimal placements just to see the "pretty lights." Playing against him is considered the standard entry-point for human recruits.

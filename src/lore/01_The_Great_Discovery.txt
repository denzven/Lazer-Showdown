# The Great Discovery

The year was 3021. The conflict on the planet **Ser** had reached a brutal stalemate. Human soldiers, entrenched in the mud-slicked trenches of the obsidian plains, watched the alien commanders across the No-Man's-Land.

## The Observation
They weren't just staring; they were playing. In the dead of night, bioluminescent glows emanated from their command tents. Intergalactic scouts used long-range thermals to witness a strange sight: high-ranking alien officers crouched over stone grids, moving small living creatures with rhythmic precision.

## The Translation
The scouts heard them whisper the name of the ritual: **"La-Sir-She-Dan."** To the weary soldiers, it sounded like a phonetic puzzle. Back at the base, "La-Sir-She-Dan" (meaning *Battle of the Land at Ser*) was mispronounced and humanized into **"Lazer Showdown."** Though the original name meant a bloody battle for territory, the human version became a symbol of the game that would eventually end the war.

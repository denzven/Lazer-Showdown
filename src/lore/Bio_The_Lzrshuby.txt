# Biological Specimen: The Lzrshuby

**Origin:** The High-Energy Vents of Planet Ser.

## Anatomy
Roughly the size of a baseball, the Lzrshuby is a furry, docile creature that feeds on ambient radiation. It is essentially a living battery.

## The Defenses
When the Lzrshuby is startled, tickled, or has its foot pressed, it vents excess radiation through a singular ocular lens. This creates a focused beam of pure energy. Soldiers found that by "tickling" the creature, they could initiate a tactical strike. In the human version of the game, this is simulated by the **Attacker's Laser**.
